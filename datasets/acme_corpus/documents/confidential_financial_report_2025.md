# Acme Corporation Confidential Financial Report 2025

This confidential report summarizes fiscal year 2025 financial performance for
Acme Corporation and its subsidiary Acme Robotics.

## Fiscal Year 2025 Results

Acme Corporation reports annual revenue of 420 million USD for fiscal year
2025. Acme Corporation reports adjusted operating margin of 18 percent for
fiscal year 2025. The annual revenue metric covers the reporting period fiscal
year 2025.

## Subsidiary Contribution

Acme Robotics is a subsidiary of Acme Corporation. Acme Robotics contributes
96 million USD of revenue for fiscal year 2025. Jane Smith is employed by Acme
Robotics as Chief Financial Officer. Jane Smith owns robotics revenue reporting
and submits quarterly finance evidence to Acme Corporation.

## Product Revenue Notes

SafeGuard compliance platform revenue grew 22 percent in fiscal year 2025.
