# Acme Corporation Public Company Overview

Acme Corporation is a global industrial manufacturer serving enterprise
customers in regulated markets. Acme Corporation operates from Madrid, Austin,
and Munich, and sells industrial automation systems to manufacturers, logistics
operators, and energy companies.

## Corporate Structure

Acme Robotics is a subsidiary of Acme Corporation. Acme Robotics builds
robotic inspection cells and embedded control software for industrial automation
systems. The subsidiary works with Acme Corporation's central product and
compliance teams.

## Public Products

Acme Corporation offers industrial automation systems. Acme Corporation also
offers the SafeGuard compliance platform, a software product used to track
policy attestations, evidence collection, and audit workflows.

## Public Governance

The Board of Directors governs Acme Corporation. Public communications state
that Acme Corporation maintains formal information governance, product safety,
and supplier conduct policies.
