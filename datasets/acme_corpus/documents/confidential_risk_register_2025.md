# Acme Corporation Confidential Risk Register 2025

This confidential risk register describes risks that may affect Acme
Corporation, Acme Robotics, and fiscal year 2025 reporting.

## Supply Chain Risk

Acme Corporation is exposed to supply chain risk. The highest-risk components
are machine-vision sensors, motor controllers, and safety relays used in
industrial automation systems. Orion Components is the supplier with the
largest concentration risk for vision sensors.

## Currency Exchange Risk

Acme Corporation is exposed to currency exchange risk because revenue is
reported in USD while a significant portion of European costs are incurred in
EUR. Currency exchange risk may affect reported earnings for fiscal year 2025.

## Robotics Delivery Risk

Acme Robotics is exposed to delivery risk when supplier lead times exceed
twelve weeks. Delivery risk may delay robotic inspection cell deployments for
enterprise customers.

## Mitigations

The risk team recommends qualifying a second source for vision sensors,
reviewing EUR hedging thresholds quarterly, and linking delivery-risk updates
to the Information Governance Policy evidence workflow.
