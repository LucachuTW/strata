# Acme Corporation Information Governance Policy

The Information Governance Policy is an internal policy governed by the Board
of Directors. The policy applies to Acme Corporation, Acme Robotics, and all
employees who handle customer, supplier, financial, or product records.

## Data Retention

All financial records must be retained for seven years. Product safety records
must be retained for ten years. Security incident records must be retained for
five years unless the legal team places a hold on the records.

## Responsible Roles

Maria Chen is employed by Acme Corporation as Chief Compliance Officer. Maria
Chen owns the Information Governance Policy. Jane Smith is employed by Acme
Robotics and must certify quarterly evidence from the robotics finance team.

## Controlled Access

Confidential financial reports may be accessed by Finance, Legal, and approved
board reviewers. Restricted board materials may be accessed only by the Board
of Directors, the Chief Executive Officer, and the General Counsel.
