# Acme Corporation Product Launch Notes

These internal launch notes summarize product work for fiscal year 2025.

## SafeGuard 2.0

Acme Corporation offers the SafeGuard compliance platform. SafeGuard 2.0 adds
automated evidence reminders, control-owner dashboards, and export packages for
external auditors. The product team expects SafeGuard 2.0 to reduce audit
preparation time for enterprise customers.

## Automation Systems

Acme Corporation offers industrial automation systems for inspection,
packaging, and quality-control lines. Acme Robotics contributes robotic
inspection cells to the automation systems portfolio.

## Launch Dependencies

The SafeGuard 2.0 launch depends on the Information Governance Policy because
customer evidence must follow retention and access-control rules. Industrial
automation launches depend on supplier readiness for vision sensors and motor
controllers.
